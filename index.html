Research Profile

As a Global Health Expert and PhD candidate at the University of Lausanne, I specialize in healthcare quality improvement and digital health interventions. My work focuses on developing evidence-based solutions to enhance pediatric care in resource-limited settings.

With extensive experience leading multidisciplinary research projects at Ifakara Health Institute and other renowned institutions, I bridge the gap between clinical practice, public health research, and technological innovation.

This Generative AI course represents my commitment to integrating advanced computational tools with global health research to create scalable, impactful solutions.

Course Overview

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity is an intensive 8-week program offered through the GRAPH Network at the University of Geneva. The course provides practical skills in:

  • No-code AI tools for research automation
  • Data analysis and visualization with AI
  • Developing custom AI assistants for academic work
  • Enhancing research communication through generative AI
